My project?

Big Pig Run

Big Pig Run game is a 3D endless runner game where players will enjoy being a Pig prince who has to exercise to lose weight for their beloved princess. Therefore, he entered the dimension gate of running according to the doctor's advice. Running for weight loss has happened.But of course, as a normal organism, it is not possible to exercise only. They also need to eat.That is reason why Pig prince had to eat while running.

Therefore, Big Pig Run is a game where you need to manage your calories well. Because your calories will decrease every time you run. and will increase when eating food. And when it's time for the doctor to make an appointment to check.You must be ready for the doctor watch your calories.If in the criteria. You will pass to the next level.

This game won the 1st runner-up of the RSU game dev competition, received a prize of 35,000 baht (including teachers).
